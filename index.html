The Life Story of An Iconic Boy

My Brief Bio

My life started in South Korea. I running around on the streets and gosh! The little kids would throw rocks at me. How dare they! Since those traumatic experiences from that moment on I vowed to growl at every little kid I would ever cross paths with that werent my owners. I do not like little kids they can be so mean. I also had a couple of bad run ins with other dogs and adult males while I was living on the streets. I am distrustful of men nd sometimes other dogs as well.

Although I have not had the easiest time as a young pup, I would not trade it in for the world. When I was around a year old I was adopted by a Canadian-South Korean family. They found me on the streets and deciede to take me back home to Canada! I could not believe the opportunity I was getting! All I have ever known was living on the streets alone but now I have multiple meals a day, endless water, multiple beds, and most importantly unconditional love.

Look at me living a life of luxury in Canada.

Hard Working

But dont be mistaken! I may live a good life but I am a hard worker. I am on call 24/7 and I Work tirelessly. I have quite the resume if I do say so myself. I think I am quite the dog.
